Who we are

AI meets career intelligence

Career Intelligence Technologies Ltd is a New Zealand-based technology company building the next generation of AI-driven career and recruitment platforms. Our systems process millions of job listings globally, using advanced language models to match, score, and generate actionable career intelligence.

Global reach

Our platforms aggregate opportunities from hundreds of sources across 73 countries, giving job seekers and recruiters a single, intelligent view of the global labour market.

AI-native architecture

Every product is built around large language models from the ground up — not bolted on as an afterthought. AI powers our scoring engines, document generation, and multi-agent search systems.

API-first design

Our career intelligence infrastructure is available to enterprise partners and third-party platforms via API, enabling organisations to embed smart job matching and career tools into their own products.

Built in New Zealand

Headquartered in Wellington, we bring a global perspective with a pragmatic, quality-driven approach to building technology that works at scale.
